About Us

Highly customized IT solutions with unbeatable support.

Mfreq is based in both Boulder, Colorado and McKinney, Texas. Mfreq software was started by Matt Osmus and Melanie Fredericks. We specialize in Linux-based solutions because we believe in both the quality and philosophy behind Open Source software. We are available for travel within the continental US.

We have over a decade of experience in creating custom software and hardware configurations for clients ranging from small not-for-profits to multi-million dollar corporations.

Our solutions are custom fitted to your company, and can include on-site server and back-up configuration, cloud-based web applications, audio streaming, virtualization and custom automation software.

We have a strong technical expertise in electrical engineering and have helped both big and small tech companies utilize their full potenial with our software consulting.
