Mfreq RF power design and services

RF Power Amplifier Design

Mfreq's expertise in RF Power amplifier design, including modeling and test, focuses on optimum power and efficiency.

We have designed RF power amplifiers for both small businesses and large corporations, giving us a unique ability to combine optimum results with resourcefulness that leads to a quick time-to-market. Our capability to provide a wide range of RF testing services including load pull characterization means we cover every phase of the rf design process, from idea to product.

Mfreq is also available for consulting at any stage of the RF design life-cycle, whether it's to analyze existing data sets or troubleshoot current RF power amplifier products.
